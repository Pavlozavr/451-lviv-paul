Domestic animals

  • Dog

The origin of the domestic dog includes the dog's evolutionary divergence from the wolf, its domestication, and its development into dog types and dog breeds. The dog is a member of the genus Canis, which forms part of the wolf-like canids, and was the first species and the only large carnivore to have been domesticated.

Species of domestic animals:

  • Dogs
  • Cats
  • Guinea pigs

  • Cat

There are many mysteries regarding the origin of the domestic cat. It is still unknown whether it originates from one or several ancestors. The wild North African Libyan or steppe cat is considered to be the original and probably the main ancestor of all breeds and varieties of the domestic cat - Felis libyca

  • pig

The guinea pig or domestic guinea pig (Cavia porcellus), also known as cavy or domestic cavy, is a species of rodent belonging to the family Caviidae and the genus Cavia.
